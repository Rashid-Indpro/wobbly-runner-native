‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I report a bug?

A: Email us at alpinetechinfo@gmail.com with your device model, OS version, app version, and a detailed description of the issue.

Q: Can I suggest new features?

A: Absolutely! We love hearing from players. Send your suggestions to our support email.

Q: My game data was lost. Can you help?

A: Game data is stored locally on your device. Unfortunately, we cannot recover lost data, but we can help troubleshoot to prevent future issues.

Q: Is Wobbly Runner free to play?

A: Yes! Wobbly Runner is completely free to play with no in-app purchases or pay-to-win mechanics.

Q: What devices are supported?

A: Wobbly Runner supports iOS and Android devices. Check the app store for minimum system requirements.
