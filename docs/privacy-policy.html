Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 3, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Wobbly Runner! This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application. Please read this privacy policy carefully.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device information (device type, operating system, unique device identifiers)
  • Game progress and statistics (high scores, achievements, gameplay data)
  • App usage analytics (session duration, feature usage, crash reports)
  • Performance data (frame rates, loading times, technical diagnostics)

2.2 Information You Provide

  • User-generated content (custom player names, if applicable)
  • Contact information (when you reach out to support)
  • Feedback and reviews

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for:

  • Providing and maintaining the game experience
  • Saving your game progress and achievements
  • Improving game performance and fixing bugs
  • Understanding user preferences and gameplay patterns
  • Providing customer support
  • Ensuring game security and preventing cheating

4. Data Storage and Security

Your game data is stored locally on your device and synced securely. We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

5. Third-Party Services

Wobbly Runner may use third-party services such as:

  • Analytics providers (for app usage statistics)
  • Crash reporting tools (for stability improvements)
  • App distribution platforms (Google Play Store, Apple App Store)

6. Children's Privacy

Wobbly Runner is designed to be family-friendly. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If you are a parent and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your data (by uninstalling the app)
  • Opt-out of data collection (limited functionality may result)

8. Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations. Game progress data is stored until you uninstall the app or request deletion.

9. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for such transfers.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
